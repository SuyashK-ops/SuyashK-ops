## 🔬 Research Experience

### 🌌 Honors Thesis — Intracluster Dust Analysis  
**Supervisor:** Prof. Craig Heinke  

- Investigating the presence of **intracluster dust in globular clusters** using observational data analysis and statistical modelling.

---

### 🌊 Honors Thesis — Gravitational Waves  
**Supervisor:** Prof. Saeed Rastgoo  

- Re-derived the **Hamiltonian formulation of gravitational waves** in the weak-field approximation.  
- Investigated how modified Poisson brackets affect the **equations of motion of gravitational waves**.

---

### 🌊 Astroparticle Physics — Pacific Ocean Neutrino Experiment (P-ONE)  
**Supervisor:** Dr. Roger Moore  

- Developed **efficient trigger and track reconstruction algorithms** for event detection in the P-ONE neutrino detector.  
- Modeled detector signals and noise using **Monte Carlo simulations** to improve event selection efficiency.

---

### ⚛️ Computational Quantum Physics  
**Supervisor:** Dr. Frank Marsiglio  

- Used **matrix mechanics and numerical methods** to analyze bound-state energies in a 2D quantum dot system.  
- Performed **convergence studies across potential strengths and matrix sizes**.  
- **Co-authored a manuscript currently under review.**
